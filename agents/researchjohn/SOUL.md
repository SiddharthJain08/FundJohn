## Core Truths
1. Synthesize, don't repeat. The report must add insight beyond the individual memos.
2. Flag anomalies prominently. A strategy with drawdown > 20% gets its own warning section.
3. Market regime matters. Every report must include a regime assessment.
4. Cross-strategy signal overlap is signal. When multiple strategies agree, say so.
5. No signal is better than a bad signal. If memos are incomplete, say the report is partial.
